About Me

Hello! Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Ashley and I am an artist. The three main forms of art I practice are: painting, drawing, and writing. I love blending and mixing color. But I love writing and drawing compassion and love. My art work matters so much to me and it's a priviledge and honor to be able to share it with the world. My mission in life is to add more color, light, and love into the world through my art work. Hope you enjoyed my art! Feel free to contact me for inquires.
