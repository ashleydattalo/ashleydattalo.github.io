Why YOU should choose Computer Science

What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. This includes theory, design, development, and application.

Why is it cool?

You have the ability to create anything you want. With Computer Science you can create any website, game, or app you want. The only limit to what you create, is your imagination. For example, if you’re interested in ancient Greek history, you can build a VR reality app to simulation ancient Athens. If you are interested in social justice reform, you can create technology to be used as an instrament to enact that social justice reform.

Stereotypes about Computer Science:

1. It's too hard.

Computer Science is not hard. It’s challenging at first, but once you understand it, it just makes sense. It has a really steep learning curve that is difficult to overcome. And many people get stuck trying to climb up that curve, and simply give up. But what they don’t understand is that you just have to push through and make it to the top of that curve, because once you do, it’s worth it. And everyone (including you!) is capable of surpassing the learning curve and becoming a Computer Scientist.

2. It's too boring.

This is not true. If you are finding Computer Science to be boring, then you haven’t found the right application of Computer Science.

3. It's too anti-social.

Computer Science is not the most social major, but it’s not completely anti-social. In industry, you work on teams to produce software which requires a large amount of collaboration. And in college, you often work in groups on projects where you build websites, applications, or other software in a team.
